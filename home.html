How It Feels When Your Boyfriend Is In Jail

First The challenges

 Its extremely hard having a boyfriend that is in jail. I never realized how hard it actually is until I started living it. You have to be all in to go through this. I miss him everyday and sometimes I don't even feel like getting up and around. I have my ups and my downs. Some days I'm doing just fine and some days I feel like I cant even get myself up out of this empty bed that I am alone in. I know one day we will be back together but until that day comes I have to be strong. Most days I wake up alone and then go to work. After work i'm back home alone. I miss sitting talking to him messaging him throughout the day. I miss feeling his touch and laughing with him and watching movies with him. Not getting to see him everyday is a extremely hard struggle. The jail that he is in we can not see each other in person at all. The only way that we can see each other is through video visits. There is finances all the way from putting money on books to paying to put time on the phone. The jail system charges a lot for everything. It is a lot like having a long distance relationship. many people cannot handle long distance because they need physical touch and they can't be away from their person. I am one of those people I hate that I cannot touch him. It breaks my heart not being able to. I know that this is just temporary but it is very hard not to be able to touch or kiss him. There are so many challenges but if you truly love this person you would jump through fire for them and would not let this break you apart. You just have to remember things like this will make your relationships much more stronger if you can wait.

Getting to Talk

 Talking to him is very difficult. I can not just pick up the phone and call him. The only way to talk to him over the phone is to wait for him to call me. Some days I do not even get a call at all. Some days I am at work and extremely busy so I am not able to take the call. When i do get to call him it is only a 15 minute call which is just not enough when you love the person. I hate that it is only 15 minutes and when I get off the phone with him I get extremely depressed because I have no idea when the next time I will get to talk to him. calls are 26 cents a minute and though you may think thats decent. Its actually not one 15 minute call is almost 4 dollars. It's worth it to hear his voice. I can download a jail messaging app to text him. However, he doesn't know when I text him until he actually checks his messages. That means that I could have sent a message and never recieve one back until hours later. Its nice that I can also send a video message or a picture if i want. This texting app costs 26 cents per message unless you want to send a picture then it goes up to 35 cents. Then there is visitation. some jails allow in-person visits but a lot of jails took that away during covid and have not brought it back. It is extremely difficult to not be able to see the person that you are in love with in person. Yes its good you can video visit but so much goes wrong. Some days the jail has issues with the internet and you miss it because they could not figure out how to get it to work. Other times I had it were the video never showed up and all that I could hear was audio. Also he was frozen one time and I waited a few minutes for it to fix but it did not so I had to close the visit and then reopen it just to see if it would fix the issue. All these things are worth it because of how much I love him but all these things are difficult to deal with.

The Waiting Game

 He is going to be in there for a while. So I am currently just trying to keep my mind busy. Working more at work, working more on the house when i am off. working out a lot more especially on days off. one problem I have is I like to eat my emotions so even when I work out more I am still gaining weight because of eating my emotions. However, I am also working on that and hoping to accomplish it very soon because I also have a weight loss goal that I would like to acheive before he gets out too. I know I can do this and our relationship will be unbreakable all I have to do is survive through the hardship. Things happen to make you stronger and I know this will make me stronger for sure. He is the love of my life and I will never give up. I cannot wait til he is done and we are reunited once again.
